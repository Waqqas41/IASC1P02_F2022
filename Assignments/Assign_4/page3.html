Quran and Sunnah

The Quran and Sunnah are the two main sources of revelation to Muslims. The Quran is regarded as the literal revealed word of Allah. It is a guidance for all of mankind, for every time and place. The Quran has been perfectly preserved since it was first revealed over 1400 years ago. Muslims also believe in the earlier scriptures like the Torah and Bible, but they have been abrogated by the Quran. The Sunnah is the words, actions, and practices of the Prophet Muhammad. It is worth noting that the Prophet Muhammad is regarded as the "living Quran" (i.e he showed us how to understand and practice the Quran). The Sunnah is also considered to be revelation as Allah says the Prophet Muhammad does not act of his own will.
